Delicious Gingerbread Recipe
Ingredients
  • 750 g all-purpose flour
  • 750 g sugar
  • 4 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 packet gingerbread spice mix (approx. 15 g)
  • 1½ packets baking powder
  • 7½ dl milk
  • 6 tablespoons neutral vegetable oil (e.g., sunflower or canola oil)
  • Optional: Powdered sugar for dusting
Instructions
1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (356°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.

2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the following dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, gingerbread spice mix, and baking powder. Stir until evenly mixed.

3. Gradually add the milk while stirring continuously. Then, mix in the vegetable oil. Stir until you get a smooth, thick batter.

4. Pour the batter onto the prepared baking tray and spread it evenly using a spatula.

5. Bake the gingerbread in the center of the oven for approximately 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

6. Remove the tray from the oven and let the gingerbread cool completely. Optional: Dust with powdered sugar before serving.

7. Cut into squares or rectangles, serve, and enjoy your homemade gingerbread!
